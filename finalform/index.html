Travelabulous

Travelabulous asked us to help improve the offering of their website for social travel planning. Working with the co-founders we were tasked with adding new features and improving existing ones that would help determine their product/market fit. This later expanded to helping with their crowd-funding efforts.

We airdropped into their existing techstack – a Node-based Angular app hosted on Microsoft Azure – and got to work. We started with integrating existing designs created by third party into the app, eventually taking on the design work ourselves so we could iterate and test ideas faster.

Travelabulous had expectations of what to offer potential users upon their crowd-funding campaign launch. We were able to help them reach those expectations and continued working with them to iterate on the feedback received from users.
